Fleet Management Guidelines

1. Vehicle Assignment Policy
   - All vehicles must be assigned through the fleet portal
   - Maximum assignment period: 12 months

2. Fuel Card Usage
   - Fuel cards are for official business use only
   - Receipts required for all purchases over $50

3. Accident Reporting
   - Report all incidents within 24 hours
   - Complete Form FM-101 within 48 hours

4. Maintenance Scheduling
   - Use the online booking system
   - Emergency repairs require manager approval
